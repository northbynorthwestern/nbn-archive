A preview of Reckless and The Rocky Horror Show
By Anna Waigand
5:10 a.m. Nov. 17 2006

    This week in NU theater, The Rocky Horror Show and Reckless. See below for showtimes and descriptions.


    RECKLESS by Craig Lucas
    Produced by Meghan Mackenzie
    Directed by Jeremy Bloom

    Reckless is the story of Rachel, a euphoric wife and mother who is pushed out of her house (through the window) after her husband tells her he has taken out a contract on her life. “Reckless” follows Rachel through her many journeys from game shows to near-death experiences.

    McCormick Auditorium
    November 16-18
    Thursday 8PM
    Friday 8PM and 11PM
    Saturday 8PM and 11PM

    Tickets $5 at the door.

    THE ROCKY HORROR SHOW
    Produced by Ryan Grossheim
    Directed by Philip Markle

    When an innocent couple wanders into the Frankenstein Place, they don’t know what they are getting into. Sweet transvestites, rose-tinted worlds, and the Time Warp transform the couple into lust-filled, sex-hungry creatures just like the rest of the residents under Dr. Frankenfurter’s roof.

    Shanley Pavilion
    November 16-18
    Thursday 8 PM
    Friday 8 PM and 11PM
    Saturday 8 and 11 PM

    Tickets $5 at the door and sold at Norris.
