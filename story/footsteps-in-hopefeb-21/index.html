Footsteps in Hope is painted on the Rock...Feb. 21
By Amanda NeMoyer
7:38 a.m. Feb. 21 2008

    The Rock finally got a new look today as Footsteps in Hope appeared this morning. Footsteps in Hope is an 8,000 meter walk that raises awareness about HIV and tries to improve healthcare in the most needy areas of the world.

    More information can be found on their Web site.
