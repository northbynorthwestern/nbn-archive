Baby monkey eats fruit plus comments about Red Pandas
By Patrick St. Michel
3:21 p.m. Nov. 20 2008

    Let’s get straight to tonight’s video: an adorable monkey eating an apple slice. Shout out to Cute Overload for posting this video first.

    Speaking of Cute Overload though, I’d never actually taken a look at the “comments” section of each post. I wish I had, because it would have given me a massive self-esteem boost about the fact that I maintain a cute animal blog. I enjoy cute animals, but these commenters FREAK OUT about them. Take the comments for this (adorable) post into consideration. A few gems:

    omg squeeeee, leftest one is proshing my mind into exploshuns.

    Is…is that English? I’m afraid to Google search some of this (proshing?).

    I want to rub my face all over them…the bellies…the tails…the faces….*splodes!

    This makes me want to set-up some sort of hotline.

    The lady in the middle is holding pandy like a cat, and it’s acting like it’s being strangled, LOL!
    “HALP!! She’s got meh! Run away! RUN AWAY!!!” :P
    Well, nobody mentioned the tongueage!

    LOL, strangulation!

    oh lawds sake, red pandas are so fricking adorable! i haven’t really seen much of them as so often it is the black & white pandas the media usually report on, not these uber-cute fuzzy-wuzzies.

    Stupid Giant Panda-leaning media. I listen to Red Panda talk radio.
