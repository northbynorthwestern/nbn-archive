5 facts about Ilana Glazer
By Paola de Varona
12:55 a.m. March 4 2017

    Ilana Glazer, co-creator and co-star in the Comedy Central series, Broad City, addressed the dancers with motivating words during Block 2. Glazer, while commonly known for her comedy work, has also acted in several independent films.

    Here are some other fast facts about DM's second celebrity video guest.

    1. She starred in an independent feature How to Follow Strangers. The film even won top prize at the Lower East Side Film Festival.
    2. Glazer made it onto the 2015 Forbes 30 under 30 list at the age of 27.
    3. She attended NYU and got a degree in psychology. Glazer even met her Broad city co-star, Abbi Jacobson, in the city during their time in an improv group.
    4. She smokes pot every day. In fact, on April 20 she threw a 4/20 party and her parents were in attendance.
    5. Glazer has her own web series on Youtube called Chronic Gamer Girl. In it she makes jokes, plays video games and just chats – sometimes bringing friends on the show.
