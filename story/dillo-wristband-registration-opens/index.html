Mayfest opens Dillo wristband registration
By Megan Fu
10:02 p.m. April 26 2015

    Registration for the best day of Spring Quarter will be officially open tomorrow.

    With just five weeks until Dillo, Mayfest is opening wristband registration tomorrow on the Norris Box Office online. Every undergraduate student is guaranteed a free wristband that is required to enter the festival. Students can also register for a $10 guest wristband starting Monday at noon continuing for six days through May 2. 

    The registration for guest wristbands will close each day once the number of wristbands for that specific day have been claimed. After this, students won't be able to access the guest registration until the following day. 

    There is no age restriction for student guests; however, not every student is guaranteed a guest due to capacity restrictions on the Lakefill. 

    Wristbands for students and guests will be available 10 days before Dillo Day. Students have to go in person, WildCARD in hand to a Norris Box Office representative to claim their wristband. 

    Students picking up a guest wristband must bring a picture or copy of the guest's photo ID that match the information on the guest registration forms.

    Prior to registration, graduated seniors and NU graduate students should first try to log in to the NBO system using their NetID and password, and if successful, follow the same aforementioned registration process. If unsuccessful, Mayfest recommends they create a general public account on NBO and use a non-Northwestern email address. After creating the account, graduated seniors and grad students will have access to student registration.

    Still have questions about the new Dillo wristband policies? Shoot an email to wristbands@dilloday.com.
