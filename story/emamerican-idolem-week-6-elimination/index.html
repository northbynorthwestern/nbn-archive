American Idol: Week 6 Elimination
By Lauren Cole
7:47 p.m. April 16 2008

    “This… is American Idol”
    Elimination time! This entire post is a disconnected spoiler, so SPOILER ALERT!
    -First off, what the heck is Paula wearing around her neck? Maybe she got her neck slit by Sweeney Todd and had to cover that up.
    -Once again the idols butcher a great song. This week was “One Sweet Day”. The guys from Boys II Men are turning in their pop idol graves.
    -Carly looks fabulous for the second straight night. She’s very in season with the glam pieces and high-waisted silhouette. Brooke makes me feel sad as usual. Syesha’s rocking the zebra weave.

    Kristy got sent home. Photo courtesy American Idol’s Web site.

    -Brooke reminds me of the peeing one (Potty Training Kelly) in the Barbie series or Skipper.
    -F-O-X: We know drama
    -Jason Castro smokes and plays bongos with Matthew McConaughey. Which is OK because they are both beautiful men.
    -They did the whole weird group thing to stretch out the drama since they don’t have enough people left for real drama.
    -Kristy Lee Cook is like a cockroach. She just won’t die.
    -Elliott Yamin looks like a caveman so he gives hope to all cavemen everywhere hoping to make it big in Hollywood. And hopefully this will not inspire the creators of the horrible caveman sitcom. Anyway, Yamin’s kind of Jim Croce-like. Except that I love him. “We miss you mom”. I feel sad now. Maybe I should take back all of the rotten things I’ve jotted down. -No, actually not.
    -As usual, David Archuleta should not be allowed to speak. And the uber-humble act is getting old. But he can sing like no other and he was the first to find out he was safe. No surprises there.
    -Look at that blingtastic microphone stand! I love Mariah Carey. She’s such an over-the-top diva. I normally hate her super revealing short dresses, but she looks fantastic tonight. I don’t know how I feel about this song. I love her 90s diva, power balladness and this new pop, “I’m trying to keep up with the Rihannas and the Beyonces” kind of sells out her amazing voice. When is the last time you’ve heard Mariah sing only 8 notes? Change octaves! You’ve got five! Finally, at the end of the song, something a little less boring. She’s always criticized for not showing vocal restraint, but I much prefer the over-the-top Mariah to her new, boring style. On a positive note, she’s got a permanent wind machine. That hair fluttering is the cause of natural disasters everywhere.

    Anyway, moving on.
    Bottom three:
    Syesha Mercado
    Kristy Lee Cook
    Brooke White
    Eliminated Contestant:
    Kristy Lee Cook. The cockroach finally died. Against all odds, I am rid of her. Unbelievable. Unbelievably satisfying. Now go find your horse and beat a few chicks up in the cage fighting circuit. You deserve it.
    Until next week, Seacrest is once again out.
