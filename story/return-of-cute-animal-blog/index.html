The triumphant return of the Cute Animal Blog
By Katherine Zhu
5:27 p.m. Jan. 31 2011

    It seems I have some pretty big shoes to fill.

    Thank goodness I have cute animals at my disposal.

    After nearly two long years, NBN’s Cute Animal Blog has finally returned. I’m going to do my best to update this blog daily, because I know how important cute animals are to everyone’s lives. It’ll make this winter (and impending #SNOMG) seem just a tad more bearable.

    (Worth mentioning here that Patrick St. Michel, our old blogger, keeps up a cute animal Twitter that has about 3,200 followers.)

    For some of you, cats in tight places may be your calling (stay tuned, cat lovers). For others, it’s puppies that make your heart melt. Well, tonight’s theme is all things cute — after all, we’ve got almost two years to catch up on.
