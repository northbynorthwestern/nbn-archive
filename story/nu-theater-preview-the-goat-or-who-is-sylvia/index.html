NU theater preview: The Goat, or Who Is Sylvia?
By Anna Waigand
1:32 p.m. Jan. 26 2007

    The Goat, or Who is Sylvia?
    by Edward Albee
    Produced by Josh Schecter
    Directed by Alex Dorsey

    The Goat or Who is Sylvia? is about Martin, an architect who is in the midst of a high point in his life. He is not only awarded a great contract and receives an international prize, but he also turns 50 all in one week. Martin’s marriage, career, and life are threatened when his family learns his secret — who Sylvia is.

    Shanley Pavilion
    Thursday, January 25th at 8 PM
    Friday, January 26th at 8 and 11 PM
    Saturday, January 27th at 8 and 11 PM

    Tickets are $5 at the door.
