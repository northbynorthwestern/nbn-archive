Homecoming's Highlights
By Alex Furuya, Devon Levy, Lucy Wang, Mia Zanzucchi, Natalie Escobar, Wei Wei
11:42 p.m. Oct. 19 2014
  • Homecoming Grand Marshal Ana Gasteyer (Communication ’89) waves at the crowd. Photo by Natalie Escobar / North by Northwestern
  • Communication senior Mary Spohn DJs Cascada's "Every Time We Touch" at Purple Fest. Photo by Mia Zanzucchi / North by Northwestern
  • Pi Beta Phi and Alpha Epsilon Pi shout to the onlookers as they throw candy at them. Photo by Alex Furuya / North by Northwestern
  • Weinberg freshman Michael Ryzhov rides a unicycle to represent Chapin Humanities Residential College. Photo by Alex Furuya / North by Northwestern
  • Students could choose between the Beavers Coffee and Donuts, Taquero Fusion, Chicago Cupcake, and Sweet Ride food trucks on Oct. 13 in front of Deering Library.​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • ​Weinberg freshmen Rachel Wallack and Cari Schuette blow the remains of their powder paint off their hands at the Color Roar on Oct. 12.​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • At the Homecoming game against Nebraska, the football team stands by at the beginning of the first quarter. Photo by Wei Wei / North by Northwestern
  • Weinberg junior Michelle Lortie performs with the NU Color Guard and NUMB at the Pep Rally.​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • Communication senior Sean Earley, this year's Homecoming King, waves to the students and alumni at Ryan Field during halftime.​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • At the Color Roar on Oct. 12, students received free sunglasses to protect their eyes and three packets of paint each to throw at their friends and any other students involved.​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • ​The Color Guard performs along with the Marching Band's musical medley.​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • Weinberg junior ​Aaron Blow and McCormick junior Evan Gray, the SpiriTeam of the Marching Band, snap a selfie following the Wildcats' first touchdown. Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • Quarterback Trevor Siemian, Communication senior, runs the ball 7 yards before being tackled by the 'Huskers.​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • ​After receiving a pass from quarterback and Communication senior Trevor Siemian, wide receiver and McCormick junior Miles Schuler runs the ball a few yards closer to a first down before being tackled.​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • ​The trumpet section puts up their claws and growls at the Cornhuskers with the rest of the student section after the first down.​​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
  • ​The student section jingles their keys with pride at the Nebraska fans and players before kickoff in the third quarter.​ Photo by Devon Levy / North by Northwestern
