These creatures are SNOW CUTE
By Katherine Zhu
5:07 p.m. Feb. 1 2011

    The Snowmageddonpocalypse has been felt all around the Midwest. Restaurants have shut down early, shuttles canceled and the City of Evanston has declared a “Snow Emergency” for this coming Thursday and Friday.

    Even Cute Animal Blog has been affected. I know promised you guys a gallery of kitteh .gifs, but in light of this blizzard, I’ve decided to change tonight’s tune, inspired by Gus Wezerek and this little guy.

    So after putting a lot of thawed into it, I’ve decided on a CUTE ANIMAL BLOG SPECIAL EDITION: “SNOW CUTE.”

    I can’t believe I neglected my heritage in my last post. Mostly cause my heritage is flippin’ cute. Exhibit A: PANDAS IN SHANXI, CHINA. This poor cutie, got so scared after a snowstorm that he climbed up a tree.
