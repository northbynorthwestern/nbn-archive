'She Kills Monsters': live-action Dungeons and Dragons
By Missy Chen
10:50 a.m. Nov. 1 2015
  • The main characters decide to venture together to find back lost souls.
  • The three friends fight together in “New Landia”, named by Tilly.
  • Communication sophomore Rachel Guth plays Farrah/Tina in the drama.
  • The drama includes characters like cheerleading succubi, five-headed dragons, half-naked monsters and other fantastical elements.
  • Brett Warner plays Chuck Biggs, the game's Dungeon Master, in the show.
  • Communication senior Mary Iris Loncto plays Kaliope/Kelly in the show.
  • Communication junior Rosie Jo Neddy plays Agnes' younger sister, Tilly.
  • Copeland plays Agnes Evans, a caring young woman looking to better understand her younger sister.
  • Communication sophomore Mariah Copeland plays the lead in "She Kills Monsters," shown here along with Communication senior Brett Warner.
Photos by Missy Chen / North by Northwestern

The Purple Crayon Players is presenting She Kills Monsters at McCormick Auditorium this week. In this stage show, the main character Agnes Evans plays her younger sister Tilly's Dungeons and Dragons adventure, trying to understand Tilly's obsession and find back what is missing. Shows are on Thursday night, Friday night and Saturday afternoon with a ticket price of $5 for students and $10 for non-students.
