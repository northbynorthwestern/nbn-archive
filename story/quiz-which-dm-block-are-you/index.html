Quiz: Which DM block are you?
By Rena Behar
4:27 p.m. March 6 2008

    With DM looming on the horizon, it’s important to get all of your priorities and extensive checklists in order. And how can you possibly go into 30 hours of dancing without knowing which block to adopt as your own? Let us help you figure out which three-hour chunk you should try and muster up the most enthusiasm for.

    We’ll start you out easy. When are you at your best?



    Describe your personal style.



    What are your spring break plans?



    Which of these artists is most likely to be on your ‘top 25 most played’ list?



    When you need to de-stress, you’re most likely to…



    Where do you see yourself in five years?



    How do you feel about pets?



    On a given Saturday night when you aren’t dancing maniacally, what are you most likely to be doing?



    You’re the very first block, “Searchlights and Showstoppers.”
    You love your pop stars and your pop culture and just aren’t happy if you aren’t kicking things off and causing a scene. We recommend you tone down the glitter and possibly put on some panties.
    You’re block 2, “Pirates, Punks and Playas.”
    You probably cheated because you wanted to be a pirate. Or you actually are one. We see right through you, matey. Yarrr..
    You’re the “STA’s Frequent Fliers” block.
    Not only are you known as the most agonizing block, but based on your answers, you’re probably kind of pretentiously international. But you’ve racked up enough frequent flier miles to take half of Northwestern to China. Ni hao!
    You’re the “Whole Foods’ You Got Served” block.
    You’re very into your food, and probably kind of a hippie, but that’s okay. The dining halls are probably killing you, though. Our apologies.
