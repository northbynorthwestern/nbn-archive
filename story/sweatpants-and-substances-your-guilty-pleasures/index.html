Sweatpants and substances: your guilty pleasures
By Shelbie Bostedt
5:49 a.m. March 8 2014

    In keeping with the theme of "Guilty Pleasures," NBN took to the DM tent to find out what you like "ironically."

    "One Direction, but I'm not guilty about it."
    Kyle Hancher, Weinberg sophomore

    "Beer."
    Alec Brumley, Weinberg senior

    "Kesha."
    Colin Repetti, Weinberg freshman

    "Sweatpants."
    Tait Murphy, Weinberg freshman

    "Shakira."
    Sam McQuillan, SESP senior

    "I'm too frat to function."
    Ethan Cohen, Medill freshman

    "Muppets and puppets!"
    Jamie Cortese, Communication junior

    "Redheads."
    Ben Terdich, Weinberg junior

    "Science!"
    Andy Donaldson, Weinberg junior

    "T. Swift."
    James Keane, SESP sophomore

    "Reality television shows."
    Samantha Fenton, Weinberg sophomore

    "Sequins!"
    Madeleine Kenyon, Communication freshman

    "Keeping Up With the Kardashians!"
    Sydney Lindsey, Weinberg sophomore
