Is this a date? NBN's guide to dating
By Mira Wang
7:52 p.m. Nov. 12 2014

    Northwestern students crowded into a too-small classroom in Harris Hall for a very special Monday-night class: How to Ask Someone Out 101.

    Members of two clubs – Sexual Health and Assault Peer Educators (SHAPE) and Men Against Rape and Sexual Assault (MARS) – participated in a panel discussion that delivered veritable gold mines of relationship advice for their unsure peers.

    But in case you skipped that class, here’s a fancy flowchart to help you navigate your relationship problems. Enjoy.
