DG/ZBT sets fundraising record at 2014 DM
By Zach Silva
12:09 a.m. March 9 2014

    DG-ZBT reclaimed their title as the highest fundraising team in the large group category with a record-breaking $154,319.34, shattering their winning total from last year. Kappa-Pike raised the second highest amount with $88,885.32 and APhi/Sigma Chi came in third with $75,371.57.

    The top fundraising group in the medium category was AXO-AEPi with a total of $31,017.49, followed by PiPhi-Delt in second with $30,512.42 and Gamma Phi-Phi Delt in third with $29,486.

    First place in the small fundraising group was awarded to Project Wildcat (PWild), which raised $18,399.94. Second place was given to the Northwestern University Marching Band (NUMB) with $17,805.95. Third place went to DZ/Lambda Chi with $17,493.60. Elder, which placed in sixth in the small group category, was given the RHA Award for highest fundraising total by a residence hall.

    A new PHA/IFC Award was also instituted for this year's Dance Marathon to honor the highest per capita funds raised among new fundraising teams. The inaugural award went to Delta Chi and Kappa Alpha Theta.

    For individual awards, the highest fundraising committee member was Sam McQuillan with $8,381.85. The highest fundraising individual was John Gallagher at $6,740.00.
