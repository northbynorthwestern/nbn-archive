Cute Animal Blog...the end???
By North by Northwestern Staff
3:41 p.m. June 1 2009

    I still remember the night Cute Animal Blog was born, mainly because I was stunned my editor gave it the green light. Even I thought the idea of a blog devoted to nothing but fuzzy critters was absolutely insane. But here I am now, months of posts later from the first one, it turned out to be a pretty great idea. But now, we’ve reached a crossroads …I’m about to graduate, and the future of Cute Animal Blog appears to be uncertain.

    Is this…dun dun dun…the end of Cute Animal Blog???

    Well, not really. In one of my more rash decisions, I’ve purchased the domain name cuteanimalblog.com because…geez, I don’t know it was cheap and I make bad Internet decisions (I’ve come dangerously close to buying this shirt on multiple occasions). So, in my head, Cute Animal Blog will continue on as its own Web site, a constantly updating place to check out adorable animals. Because what the web needs is another place to look at cats. I’ve already tricked several people into wanting to contribute to the site, now all I need is an actual, functioning Web page! Keep your fingers crossed and look for the site to launch sometime this summer (while you wait, keep following our Twitter, it will update!). Let’s celebrate this news with a puppy!

    But tonight does end a major chapter in Cute Animal Blog’s history. This will be the last post ever on NBN, and this blog’s run on the site has been pretty great. I could wax poetic on Cute Animal Blog and my time running it, but I’ll keep it simple and say one of the biggest perks of my day the past two years has been seeking out cute animals to share with you.
