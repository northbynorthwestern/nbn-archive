Freshman Guide 2010: where to eat, where to go, how to get by
By North by Northwestern Staff
7:54 p.m. Sept. 16 2010

    Freshman year is a rough and swift lesson in that great task of adulthood: figuring out what the hell to do with your time. Here, North by Northwestern writers have compiled their best advice for living a good freshman life — from doing the laundry to dating to finding a sense of purpose.
